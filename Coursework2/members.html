Members



We have a range of archers within our club including a mix of third, second and first years as well as a range of demographics. We are a very inclusive club so no matter what background you have you are welcome to shoot as long as you have completed the beginneers course. Our current Chairperson is Cameron who has encorged and improved a range of equipment and tournement arangements and we have tons of member competitions including nerves of steel. Our membership includes access to the watershed training sessions as well as invitations to all BUCS competitions some of which take us to Warwick, Cambridge and loughbrough.

About us



We are students at De Montfort university(DMU) who come together for the joint sport of Archery. We train twice a week on Tuesday's 16:30 - 18:30 and Sunday 13:00 - 17:00.

Within each session we continue to improve our skills in archery with our lead Chairperson Cameron and hope to improve our rankings in the Butt's league which is our competition programme where we compete with other university's across the country including Warwick University and Cambridge University.

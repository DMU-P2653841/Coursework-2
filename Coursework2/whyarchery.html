Why Join



Archery is a fantastic sport that few have the opportunity to really get to have a go at. Here at DMU Archery we support and encourge everyone to just give it a go and have fun with it. Whether that be missing the target or becoming the next olympic athlete. We put on a fantastic beginneers course at the start of the academic year which allows all new potential members to be able to sign up and start shooting. So why not give it a go.
